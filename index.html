我的研究方向主要集中在分布式机器学习、金融科技与安全、计算机网络。目前，研究成果共撰写了60余篇论文，分别发表在SIGCOMM、EuroSys、ICDE、SIGMETRICS、AAAI、TON、INFOCOM等国际顶级学术期刊和会议上；共申请13项专利，获得3项专利授权。作为主要作者编写专著1部。本人现担任国家重点研发计划课题、国家自然科学基金、广东省重点领域研发计划课题等6项国家/省部级项目负责人。本人现担任中国计算机学会互联网专委会执行委员、数字金融分会执行委员、ACM SIGCOMM China专委会委员；获得2022年SIGCOMM中国新星奖，2020年上海市“超级博士后”激励计划；本人担任包括TON、INFOCOM、KDD、TPAMI在内的多项国际顶级期刊及国际顶级会议审稿人和技术方案委员会委员。

Me in a Nutshell

Sen Liu      Associate Professor
senliu[at]fudan.edu.cn [DBLP] [ORCID] [Google Scholar]
College of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence / Institute of Financial Technology, Fudan University

My research interests broadly include distributed machine learning, FinTech security architectures, and next-generation computer networks. I have published more than 60 papers, including top-tier conferences and journal like SIGCOMM, EuroSys, ICDE, SIGMETRICS, AAAI, TON, and INFOCOM, holding more than 10 patents. Currently, I am the PI of 5+ provincial and national key research projects. My professional service includes executive committee member in Technical Committee of CCF-TCI, CCF Digital Finance, and ACM SIGCOMM China. I won the ACM SIGCOMM China Rising Star Award 2022, and Shanghai Super Postdoctoral Incentive Plan, 2020. I served as a TPC member or a reviewer for many international conferences and journals, including TON、INFOCOM、KDD、TPAMI.

Research Interests

  • #1：Distributed Machine Learning Training and Inference
  • #2：Network Performance Optimization in Datacenter Network / Intelligent Computing Center
  • #3：Financial Data Security and Data Mining
I am looking for self-motivated Ph.D, master students, and undergraduate interns with rich experience in coding and programming. If you are interested in working with me in the operating of distributed systems, optimization of network stack and OS kernels, tabular data learning and synthesis, time series data prediction, and quantitative trading, please drop me an email with your CV.
